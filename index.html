WE LOVE
COCKTAILS

  • Where does this love come from?

    To be honest the passion I got for cocktails didn't originate from me, but from a mate of mine. He started to experiment with the differents kinds of liqours and ways to put them together. But for one to experiment, one also needs a test rabbit to know if it is a good cocktail or not. That is where I come in.

  • Drunk too many times?

    For a test rabbit as myself, a test moment wouldn't just be a moment to test 1 new recipe. No no no, on such occasion i would be bombarded with a bunch of new recipes to try. But this moments are still really fun, for as the bartender gets drunk with you. So to say if at this moment i wouldn't be in love with these drinks, i would after what comes next.

  • What does it yield?

    Up at the moment of testing only a few evenings getting wasted. Not much later after my mate becoming a bartender at the best cocktailbar in Utrecht. The occasional free drink, and crazy stories of his colleagues. But to see where the spark of cocktail love electrified me. I will tell you a crazy story.

  • This love = opportunity!

    While on a way in the far lands of Austria, we encountered an opportunity. A Burger Boutique that wanted to sell high quality cocktails, got my mate and I as their customers. After a good conversation, we made a deal to give them a workshop. And without them knowing a newly designed menu. So i my mates way of his soon to be proffesion, and my way of way design. I totally fell in love with these magical drinks.
